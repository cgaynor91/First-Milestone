About Us

Your Performance-Driven Partner

From multi-national firms to private clients, and from local authorities to historical buildings – John Gaynor Construction is the strength behind the building construction industry in Ireland’s Mid West. And we’re ready to bring our skill, professionalism and unquestioned reputation to your project.
John Gaynor Construction is conveniently based in Ennis, Clare and is a performance-driven company specialising in contract work as well as being a competitive force in the tender market.
The foundation of our business is our simple but vital combination of cost-effectiveness, quality, reliability and timely delivery – assured with every project we undertake.

Our highly experienced team has extensive knowledge in the healthcare, commercial, industrial, educational, residential and civil engineering sectors, and has completed projects from minor building works to major construction projects.
At John Gaynor Construction, we work very closely with our clients and their design teams to ensure customer satisfaction is achieved above all. Our reputation for quality construction is borne out by the number of repeat orders from clients satisfied with our company’s performance.
Providing client satisfaction is the key to securing repeat business and that is why we at Conack, work tirelessly to provide proactive, innovative and flexible solutions to all projects.
